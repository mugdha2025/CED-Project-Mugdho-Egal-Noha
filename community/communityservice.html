Community Service

Welcome to our Community Service Page. Here, you'll find a diverse range of opportunities to engage in meaningful service and make a positive impact in the Cambridge community. Whether you're passionate about environmental conservation, social justice, or education, there's something for everyone to get involved in. Explore the various activities and initiatives listed below to find the perfect fit for your interests and schedule.

Get Involved

There are many ways you can get involved in community service at CRLS:

  • Volunteer at local shelters or food banks
  • Participate in neighborhood clean-up events
  • Join clubs or organizations focused on community service
  • Organize fundraising events for charitable causes
  • Mentor younger students in academic or extracurricular activities

Key and Popular Community Service Activities

  • Club 4
  • National Honors Society (NHS)
  • UNICEF
  • Cambridge Volunteers
